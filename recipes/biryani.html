Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani is a delicious and fragrant Pakistani/Indian rice dish.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Ground Cumin
  • Chili Powder
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Ground Tumeric
  • Plain Yogurt
  • Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Cardamom
  • Cinnimon Stick
  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice
  • Cloves
  • Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in skillet. Fry potatoes until lightly browned. Remove potatoes and set aside.
  3. To skillet, add onions, garlic, and ginger then cook until onions are soft and golden. Add tomatoes, salt, cumin, chili powder, pepper and turmeric. Cook and stir constantly.
  4. Stir in yogurt, mint, cardamom and cinnimon. Cover and cook over low heat. Stir occasionally and add a little water if needed.
  5. Add chicken and stir well to coat. Cook until chicken is tender and gravy is thick.
  6. Meanwhile, cook the rice.
